Jean is a family feature film by Gregory P. Wolk about a teenage girl who, not exactly thrilled at being her grandparent’s caregiver, is surprised to find excitement and fulfillment in unlikely places. Shot in beautiful Sedona, Arizona, the film is in the final stages of post-production.
